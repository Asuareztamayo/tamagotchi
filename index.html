A week ago we were asked
to make a tamagotchi,
and this is the result.
To play follow the instructions below

To keep him/her alive you have to be aware of his/her needs of food, tiredness and boredom.

If any of the needs go up to 5, your character will die.

Click on the image of the need you want to give to the character.

If the needs are maxed out, you will not be able to give them more.

Click on the coffin to restart the game
